About Us

Up All Night is an all purpose entertainment portal. We are here to guide you to the best events in the Lejweleputswa region.
We have the latest entertainment news, our photo gallery is filled with photos from the weekends major events, updated every Monday. Beyond just club lights and copious embibement we also offer you a plethora of adventuer options
Only place in the district where you can stay abreast of the latest trends in style and threads. We are not all about the vanity though, we endeavour to feed your mind too. Our reviews sections has the deets on which books, movies or music to be getting into.

Our primary target market is the upwardly mobile urban youth, 18 - 35 years, with a sense of adventure, style and a certified go-getter. And that's exactly what we offer our advertisers.
Up All Night is a completely youth and black owned company founded by true natives of the Lejweleputswa district.
